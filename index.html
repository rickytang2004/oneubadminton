What is ONEU?

We've created a dedicated singles social designed for players who want to compete, improve, and connect through 1 on 1 badminton singles gameplay.

All Levels Are Welcome!
Whether you're new to singles or an advanced player, there's a place for you here.
Rules & Match Format
21 points per match, then rotate to your next opponent.
Match Making (Pick & Play)
When you're off court, choose an opponent of your choice. If you're new, the ONEU Team will help match you with a player of similar level.

Personal Note

Why I Started ONEU

Creating the ONEU Sydney Singles Social was inspired by my students. They often told me they didn't have any chances to play singles and I wanted to give everyone a place to play and be competitive — not just inside of training. I want it to be a fun yet competitive space where players can grow, have fun and improve. So come along and join us!
— Ricky
Host, ONEU Sydney Singles Badminton
